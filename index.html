About

I've been doing Discord community management for about three years, mostly for Roblox studios, content creators, and their communities. The ones I've run have gone from a couple thousand members up to around 84,000.

Day to day that's onboarding, running staff teams, engagement, announcements, partnerships, and the game-side coordination that ties it together. I don't do much hands-on moderation myself, it's more the higher-level side.

About a year and a half ago I added live ops: running in-game events, handling the Discord side, watching analytics, and coordinating across teams. On top of that I've set up 150+ servers, paid and my own, and spent the last two years building bots like North Bot.
